Safety Control Systems

Brake Plausibility Device (BSPD)

Project Overview

Designed an independent safety monitoring circuit to systematically drop the tractive system shutdown loop when structural non-plausibility limits (simultaneous high-power throttle and heavy braking actions) are systematically reached.

Key Features & Capabilities

  • Analog Imbalance Verification: Deploys ultra-low propagation delay operational amplifiers to detect input anomalies.
  • Non-Programmable Safety: Constructed purely via hardwired analog logic configurations to meet stringent non-programmable vehicle safety compliance demands.
  • Robust Noise Rejection: Built-in hardware filtering steps to isolate signal anomalies away from high-noise vehicle motor control lines.
